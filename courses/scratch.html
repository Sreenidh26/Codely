Course Description

This course offers an introduction to Scratch, a coding website for beginning programmers. We will cover all of the basics of Scratch in this course and assist each young programmer develop a passion for coding. We'll go through each component of Scratch one by one, then have two projects at the conclusion of the course for students to demonstrate their understanding. Motion and Events, Looks and Sounds, Control and Sensing, Variables and Lists, and Operators are all included in Scratch.

Who

Students Ages 6 to 14 Who

  • Have Never Used Scratch Before
  • Have Minimal Experience in Scratch

Purpose

Through Codely we hope to provide free, accessible, and high quality coding classes. This is a great alternative than expensive programming classes!

Created By

Sreenidh Bonagiri who strives to make education more equitable. He has expertise in web development, game development and app development, as well as a passion for education and learning.

Values

I am responsible for the students I teach, giving them the highest quality education possible. I value integrity, privacy, and honesty in my classes and workplace.

Structure

In order to give an individualized experience, each class is limited to 10-15 students. Each class will be held weekly over Zoom with course materials posted in Google Classroom. Once a course is full a waitlist will be created.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the price for a class?

    Free! Because we are a non-profit company, our program is completely free of charge.

  • Who can sign-up?

    Anyone! Because our classes are free, anybody and everyone may participate or learn a new programming language.

  • What platform do you use for classes?

    We utilize Google Classroom to upload class materials, give assignments, and make critical announcements in our Zoom classes.

  • Are you looking for new teachers?

    Yes, Codely is constantly on the lookout for fresh professors and organizers. Please fill out the teacher application forms if you are interested in collaborating with us!

  • What do you teach?

    Programming and coding are the emphasis of our classes. We provide a wide range of computer science courses, and there will be more in the future!

  • How many classes do you have?

    Currently we offer 3 unique and well-crafted courses and are always looking towards expanding our selection.

  • How long are your classes?

    Our classes range from 45 minutes to an hour long.

  • Is there homework for students?

    Yes, teachers will assign homework to students each week, which they must complete and submit. We'll grade and go through the assignment in class the following week.
